Our Story

From the Coast of Merka to Lake Street

Welcome to Quruxlow Restaurant, your home for authentic East African cuisine in the heart of East Lake Street, Minneapolis. We take pride in serving freshly prepared traditional dishes made with love and rich cultural flavor.

Our recipes come from the coastal city of Merka, near Mogadishu, celebrating heritage, community, and hospitality. From our famous Halwo Sweet to the sizzling Chicken Kaykay, we serve generous portions prepared with traditional spices and the finest halal ingredients.
